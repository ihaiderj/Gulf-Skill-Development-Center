Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses, admissions, and services

Do you provide job placement?

We provide guidance and support for job applications, interviews, and documentation, but we do not guarantee job placement. Our focus is on providing quality training and skill development to prepare you for Gulf employment opportunities.

Are certificates valid abroad?

Yes, our certificates are accepted by employers for skill verification. They demonstrate your training and competency in the specific trade, which is valuable for Gulf job applications and trade tests.

What is the duration of courses?

Most courses are 30‚Äì90 days depending on the skill level and complexity. Basic courses like Driving and Hospitality are typically 30-45 days, while technical courses like Electrical, Welding, and AC & Refrigeration are 60-90 days.

Do you prepare for Gulf trade tests?

Yes ‚Äî we prepare candidates for trade tests in Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman & Bahrain. Our training includes mock tests, hands-on practice, and preparation aligned with GAMCA, SASO, and employer-specific standards.

What documents are required for admission?

You need a passport-size photo, Aadhar card copy, educational certificates, and passport (if available). You can upload these documents through our online application form.

What are the class timings?

We offer flexible batch timings: Morning (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM), Afternoon (1:00 PM - 4:00 PM), and Evening (5:00 PM - 8:00 PM). You can select your preferred timing during the admission process.

Do you provide placement support?

Yes, we provide comprehensive placement support including documentation assistance, trade test booking guidance, interview preparation, resume writing, and ongoing support throughout your job search process.

Are the courses practical or theoretical?

Our courses emphasize hands-on, practical training. We have modern training labs and workshops with real equipment where you'll practice the skills needed for Gulf employment. Theory is kept minimal and focused on practical application.

What is the fee structure?

Course fees vary depending on the program. We offer affordable fees with best-in-class training. Please contact us or fill out the application form to get detailed information about fees for specific courses.
