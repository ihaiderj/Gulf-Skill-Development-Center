About Gulf Skill Development Center

Gulf Skill Development Center (a unit of Gulf Education & Training Foundation) provides world-class technical training and Gulf-oriented skill development. Our programs prepare candidates for trade tests, international employment, and high-growth technical careers.

We offer technical, industrial, and vocational courses designed to meet international job standards. Our trainers, labs, and curriculum ensure that every candidate receives hands-on training and real-world exposure before they appear for trade tests or join foreign employers.
